Syllabi conflicts and unclear dates.
  • Anatomy MSK Exam 1 date conflict

    Orientation slides say Sep 14. Anatomy syllabus + Biochem say Sep 15. Calendar currently uses Sep 15.

  • Anatomy MSK Exam 2 date conflict

    Orientation slides say Oct 5. Anatomy syllabus + Biochem say Oct 6. Calendar currently uses Oct 6.

  • Anatomy / Biochem Cardiopulmonary Exam 1 date unknown

    Anatomy syllabus: Oct 12. Biochem + Anatomy Orientation: Oct 19. Not shown on the calendar until confirmed.

  • Anatomy Renal Exam date conflict

    Orientation slides say Dec 7. Anatomy syllabus body + Biochem say Dec 9 (one syllabus line also says 12/9/25). Calendar currently uses Dec 9.

  • Anatomy Year typos in syllabus

    Several dates are written as 2025 (e.g. 10/8/25, 11/10/25, 12/9/25). One lab date is written 9/18/22 but labeled Friday — Sep 18, 2026 is a Friday, so that lab (gluteal / posterior thigh) is included as 2026. Confirm if unsure.

  • Biochem Oct 2 holiday mislabel

    Syllabus says “Rosh Hashanah (classes until 12 PM)” on Oct 2. Actual holiday that day is Sukkot ending / Hoshana Rabbah. Unclear if early dismissal still applies.

  • Physiology No calendar dates in syllabus

    Only week numbers / topics. No interactive-session or exam calendar dates. Not plotted until faculty confirm shared modular exam dates.

  • OMM Written Exam 2 date conflict

    Course Layout: Fri Nov 20. Syllabus Exam Dates: Nov 23 (also GI Exam day). Not shown until confirmed. Practicals and Written Exam 1 are clear.

  • OMM Minor year typos in Course Layout

    Week 4 listed as 08/25/2025; one lab as 09/24/25. Treated as 2026.

  • PME Two sessions on Sep 29

    Impaired Physicians at 10–11 AM and Four Quadrant Approach at 3–4 PM both listed. Only the 10–11 AM session is on the calendar.

  • PME Two topics both listed Oct 27 at 10–11 AM

    Inter-professional Education and Confidentiality/HIPAA share the same slot. Neither is on the calendar.

  • PME Optional exam review on Thanksgiving

    Syllabus lists Nov 26 for optional review — that is Thanksgiving / no classes. Likely canceled or moved.

  • PD Written exams say “see school calendar”

    Syllabus also lists Written Exam 1 Sep 28 and Written Exam 2 Dec 7. Those dates are used, but still worth confirming with Dr. Giovanis.

  • PD Campus schedule differences

    Some sessions list different dates for Harlem/Montana vs Middletown (e.g. first week Aug 5 Middletown vs Aug 12 Harlem). Calendar follows Middletown.

  • Jewish holidays / classes Some Biochem supplementals fall on holiday eves or Chol HaMoed

    Confirmed on calendar: Biochem supplemental on Sep 11 (Erev RH, until 12), Sep 25 (Erev Sukkot, until 12), Oct 2 (Sukkot ends), and Dec 4 (Chanukah begins at sundown). Full no-class only clearly stated for Yom Kippur. Sukkot mid-week (Sep 28–Oct 1) still has exams/classes.
